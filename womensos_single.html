Women SOS Application

  • GitHub Repository

For users, our application provides a range of functionalities aimed at increasing personal safety and facilitating quick access to assistance in times of need. From real-time location tracking and emergency SOS alerts to discreet distress signals and trusted contact notifications, our platform equips women with the tools they need to navigate their surroundings with confidence and peace of mind.

In addition to its emergency response capabilities, our application serves as a hub for resources and information related to women's safety. Users can access educational content, self-defense tips, and legal resources to enhance their knowledge and awareness of safety issues. Furthermore, our platform facilitates community support through features such as safety networks, where users can connect with other women in their area for mutual aid and solidarity.
